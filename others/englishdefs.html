English Definitions

Who was Shakespeare?
English poet and dramatist considered one of the greatest English writters(1564-1616)



What is Vocabulary?
  1. A language of a user's knowledge of words
  2. The system of techniques or symbols serving as a means of expression (as in arts or crafts)
  3. A listing of the words used in some enterprise



What is hybridize?
Breed of animals using parents of different races and varities.



What is Cambodian?
  1. A native or inhabitant of Combodia
  2. Of or relating to or characteristic of Combodia or it's people or language.



What is an Idiom?
  1. The style of a artist or school or movement.
  2. A manner of speaking that is natural to native speakers of a language
  3. An expression whose meanings cannot be inferred from the meanings of the words that make it up.



What is a Lexicon?
  1. A language of a user's knowledge of words.
  2. A reference book containing an alphabetical list of words with information about them.
