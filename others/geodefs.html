Geography Definitions

What is Cartography?
The making of maps and charts



What is Meterology?
  1. The earth science dealing with phenomena of the atmosphere(especially weather)
  2. Predicting what the weather will be



What is Economic Geography?
The branch of geography concerned with the production and distrubution of commodities.



What is Calculus?
  1. The branch of mathematics that is concerned with limits and with the differentiation and intergation of functions.
  2. A hard lump produced by the mineral salts; found in hollow organs or ducts of the body.
  3. An incrustation that forms on the teeth and gums.



What is Topography?
  1. The configuration of a surface and the relations among it's man-made and natural features.
  2. Precise detailed study of the surface features of a reigon



What is Geomorphology?
The branch of geology that studies the characteristics and configuration and evolution of rocks and land forms.



What is Geopolitics?
The study of the effects of economic geography on the powers of the state.



What is Fauna?
  1. The living organism characterized by voluntary movement.
  2. All the animal life in a particular reigon or period.
