Main objective of Cricket

The main objective of cricket is that make your batting last long and you have a big chance of scoring more runs. In a nutshell, it means: DO NOT GET OUT. Try to keep the ball in the control of your bat. One silly or the "I-did-it-by-mistake" type of mistake and you are already going direct to the dressing room. Even while bowling, take your concerntration where you want to throw the ball. Or else it might result in some injuries or just wrong bowling. And once out, you are out. You never have a second chance here(except for the next match), and even though you make a 100 runs or so, don't let overconfidence get the better of you. As I mentioned before, One silly or the "I-did-it-by-mistake" type of mistake and everything's finished. Many players seem to be more aggresive and serious about this game. overconfidence gets them, and they are directly to the dressing room.
