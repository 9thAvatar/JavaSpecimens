How I started writing Poems


Hello guys I'm Vishvajit and this is one of the updates I've listed in the homepage(please refer to the homepage). So I like writing poems. How did I start writing poems? Let me tell you.



Well it was a fine morning and I was attending English class. The teacher had just finished reading the chapter and was doing the book excercises. And at this point, it so happened that there was a question asking you to write a poem about an Indian Summer. Technically there was already a poem for an example. it went something like this:

Hot Days
Humid Afternoons
Pleasant Evenings
Cool Nights
Make an Indian summer!
Using my brain and full patience, in class time, was born my first ever poem. Practically writng poems is not at all hard. You just need 3 things.
  1. Imagination
  2. A topic to write on
  3. Patience
