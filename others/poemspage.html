Farmer Poem

In the hot days,
A man works in his field,
Till the humid afternoons.

It is only the pleasant evenings does he rest; in the cool nights does he sleep

He works day and night, not caring about life's plight, who else but the hardworking farmer


His fruits so sweet, his vegetables so healthy, for this is what he works for, day and night, who else but the hardworking farmer.


There goes summer, here comes winter, where he rests for the coming summers, who else, but the hardworking farmer.

There goes winter, here comes summer again. Working hard in his field of hope, with his heart feeling as if sliding down a slope.

He is always filled with support, who else, but the hardworking farmer.
