Coffe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup water
  • 1 to 2 Teaspoons Instant Coffee Powder
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons Sugar

Prepartion Steps

  1. Boil half cut of water and pour it to a mug
  2. Add 1 to 2 teaspoons of instant coffee powder to a mug
  3. Add teaspoon of sugar and mix well
  4. Add a 1 cup milk and mix again
  5. Stir your coffee and serve it
