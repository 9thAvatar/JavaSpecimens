How this system works

As this car works on sound waves it needs something to power it. Just simple!
You'll need a speaker system or anything else as long it makes sound without you making any effort.
Connect a type of hollow pipe into the Sound Tank, then at the open end of the pipe and put the speaker system inside it.
If it is a big speaker, no need to worry! The pipe's end will have closing lids where you can close them just in case dust gets into them and they malfunction
And I said "malfunction" because the pipe will have it's own system!! Close the open end of the pipe with the lid,
and then connect your device with the pipe's systems. Play music and done!!
Here, the car's systems focus on the loudness and pitch of the sound. The higher the loudness the higher the fuel capacity. the more the pitch, the more pitch, the more fuel consomption
So just add the loudness to 50% and the pitch to 50% as well and you are ready to go!!
